What We Do

Nani is a technology platform that provides structured access to selected asset-based opportunities through third-party partners.The platform does not hold participant funds or execute transactions directly.

Platform Access

We provide structured access to selected opportunities, with clear presentation of underlying assets, participation frameworks, and partner involvement.

Structured participation Opportunity clarity Defined frameworks

Partner-led delivery

Onboarding, asset handling, and execution are carried out by selected third-party partners, with clear separation between platform access and partner responsibilities.

Third-party execution Clear role separation Partner responsibility

Not a bank

Nani does not operate as a bank, lender, or regulated investment manager.

Platform model No custody of funds Partner-led structure
