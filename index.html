What We Do

Nani Fintech Plus is a facilitator platform. We connect eligible users and businesses with selected ethical, asset-backed opportunities offered through vetted partners. We do not act as a bank, lender, or investment manager.

Facilitator

We focus on introducing and structuring access to selected opportunities in a clearer and more user-friendly way.

Introduction Structured access User clarity

Partner-led delivery

Where onboarding, asset handling, or transaction execution sits with a partner, that should be made clear in the user journey.

Partner process Execution clarity Defined journey

Not a bank

Nani is not presenting itself as a bank or regulated investment firm at this phase. The website should stay focused on this facilitator role.
